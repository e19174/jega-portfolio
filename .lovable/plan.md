

# Portfolio Website for Jegatheesan Shanthakumar

A modern, dark-themed single-page portfolio website showcasing your Computer Engineering background, projects, and skills.

---

## Design & Theme
- **Dark background** (deep navy/charcoal) with **teal/blue accent colors**
- Clean, minimal, professional aesthetic with modern sans-serif typography (Inter)
- Smooth scroll navigation with a sticky top navbar
- Subtle hover animations on cards, buttons, and icons
- Fully responsive — optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile
